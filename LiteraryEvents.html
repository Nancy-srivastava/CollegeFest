Literary Events


1. Debate: Debate competition that fosters critical thinking, communication skills, and civil discourse among participants. Teams will compete in thought- provoking debates on a variety of topics, showcasing their ability to articulate arguments, and persuade audiences.
Time Limit: 1 hour. ; Team Limit: Individual or 12 teams of 2 members
2. Skit(Nukkad Natak): Skit competition where participants bring stories to life through drama, humor, and creativity. Teams will compete in theatrical performances that entertain, inspire, and captivate audiences with their storytelling and performance skills.
Time Limit: 7-10 mins. ; Team Limit: 5 teams
3. Poetry: poetry competition that celebrates the art of poetry and provides a platform for poets to share their work, connect with fellow enthusiasts, and inspire others through the power of words.
Time Limit: 2 mins only (per person). ; Participants Limit: 10 students (Individual)
